Fragments of Humanity

This collection interrogates how physical appearance influences empathy, acceptance, and interpersonal dynamics.By distorting human features into fractured yet recognizable forms, the project invites viewers to confront their biases and question the role of aesthetics in shaping our relationships and societal structures.
